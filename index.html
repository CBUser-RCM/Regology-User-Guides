Adding LROs to the LRO Inventory

Regology allows you to add new Legal and Regulatory Obligations (LROs) to the LRO Inventory during the processing workflow.

Goal: Keep our LRO Inventory evergreen & compliant.

Training time: 3-5 minutes.

1. STEP 1: Mark as Applicable or Following

When an alert is Became Law or Final Rule, you must determine if it should be added to the inventory.

Action: Select "Applicable" or “Following” for the workflow status. This action triggers the Impact Assessment.

[IMAGE 1: Regology Workflow Status Radio Buttons]

Impact Assessment Trigger:

[IMAGE 2: Impact Assessment Pop-up with buttons]

2. STEP 2: Complete the Impact Assessment

The pop-up and Initial Impact Assessment screen must be completed:

  • Select the Impacted Team.
  • Add a description of implementation work in the comments field.

DO NOT MARK AS CLOSED YET!

You will return to screen 1 - the Overview Page - for the final step.

Initial Impact Assessment Screen:

[IMAGE 3: Initial Impact Assessment Screen (Ensure this image is annotated with a red line through "Mark as in Process" and a red circle around "Impacted Team" and the Comments field, as per the guide's instruction.)]

3. STEP 3: Add to Library & Metadata

Click the Add to Library button.

Metadata: Please include all required metadata:

  • Select the Category and Entity from the dropdown fields.
  • The Additional Information field should only be completed if the LRO has global or extraterritorial application.

Add to Library Screen:

[IMAGE 4: Screenshot of the 'Add to Library' button clicked, revealing the Metadata fields (Category, Entity, Additional Information)]

4. Assignment & Submission

Assign Legal DRI and Legal Review Team for ongoing monitoring of the LRO. In most cases, this will be you and your team.

Click Finish Adding to submit.

Final Assignment and Submission:

[IMAGE 5: Screenshot showing the Legal DRI, Legal Review Team fields, and the 'Finish Adding' button]

Note:

  • Submission sends the information to RCM for review.
  • After this, go back to screen 2 and Mark the Alert Closed.

You're done!

This process is quick and simple—thanks for helping keep our LRO Inventory up to date.

If you have questions, contact the RCM team.
