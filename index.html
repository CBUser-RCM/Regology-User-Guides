Adding LROs to the LRO Inventory

Regology allows you to add new Legal and Regulatory Obligations (LROs) to the LRO Inventory during the processing workflow.

Goal: Keep our LRO Inventory evergreen & compliant.

Training time: 3-5 minutes.

STEP 1

Mark as Applicable or Following

When an alert is Became Law or Final Rule, you must determine if it should be added.

Action: Select "Applicable" or “Following” for the workflow status. This action triggers the Impact Assessment.

Image 1: Select Applicable or Following

Image 2: Impact Assessment Trigger

STEP 2

Complete the Impact Assessment

The pop-up (Screen 2) and the **Initial Impact Assessment** must be completed.

  • **Select team** from the dropdown field.
  • **Add description** of implementation work in the comments field.
DO NOT MARK AS CLOSED YET!

*After completing the assessment, you must return to Screen 1 - the Overview Page - for the final step.*

(NOTE: Image must show red line through "Mark as in Process" and red circle around "Impacted Team" and "Comments")

STEP 3

Click the Add to Library Button

Once on the Overview Page, click the Add to Library button to begin capturing the LRO's metadata.

Metadata Requirements:

  • **Include all required metadata.**
  • Select the **Category** and **Entity** from the dropdown fields.
  • The **Additional Information** field should only be completed if the LRO has **global or extraterritorial application**.
STEP 4

Assignment & Submission

Assign the **Legal DRI** and **Legal Review Team** for ongoing monitoring of the LRO. In most cases, this will be you and your team.

Action: Click Finish Adding to submit the LRO to the inventory.

IMPORTANT NOTES:

  • Submission sends the information to RCM for review.
  • **Final Step:** Go back to Screen 2 (the Alert) and **Mark the Alert Closed**.

You're Done!

This process is quick and simple—thanks for helping keep our LRO Inventory up to date.
